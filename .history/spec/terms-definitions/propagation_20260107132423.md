[[def: propagation, propagating, Propagation]]

~ Propagation is the process of creating new plants from existing ones. Methods include seed germination, cuttings, division, layering, and [[ref: Grafting]]. Each method has advantages depending on the plant species and desired outcomes.

~ Successful propagation requires understanding plant biology and providing appropriate conditions for root and shoot development. Many gardeners propagate plants to preserve desirable characteristics or multiply valuable specimens. See also [[ref: Seedling]], [[ref: Germination]], [[ref: Soil]].
